Frequently Asked Questions

How many Grands Prix did Brabham win?

Brabham won 35 Grands Prix from 394 starts. They also scored 40 poles, 42 fastest laps, 120 podiums and 832 points.

How many World Championships did Brabham win?

Brabham won the Constructors' Championship in 1966 and 1967. They won four Drivers' Championships with Jack Brabham (1966), Denny Hulme (1967) and Nelson Piquet (1981 and 1983).

Who is the most successful Brabham driver?

Nelson Piquet won 13 Grands Prix and two Drivers' Championships with Brabham.

Which engine suppliers did Brabham have during their time in Formula 1?

Brabham had seven engine suppliers: Climax, Repco, Ford, Alfa Romeo, BMW, Judd and Yamaha.

When was Brabham's debut Grand Prix and their last?

Brabham debuted at the 1962 German Grand Prix. Their last race was the 1992 Hungarian Grand Prix
